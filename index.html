About AeroSave

Smart water savings
without expensive renovations.

AeroSave helps hotels reduce water consumption by installing high-efficiency aerators in faucets and showers. Our aerators mix air into the water stream, delivering the same comfortable pressure while using up to 60% less water.

There are no expensive renovations, no plumbing changes. Installation takes minutes per faucet — and the savings start from day one.

  • Lower water bills
  • Reduced sewer / drainage costs
  • Lower energy use for hot water
  • A smaller environmental footprint
  • Improved sustainability credentials for guests
